Built to support real work

Faithful to the method

Every column, question, and equilibration check-in follows the authentic Demartini Method — TAI discipline, vector specificity, per-item answer boxes.

One box per item

Each column gives you a separate answer box for every item you list — the way the method is meant to be worked.

Autosave

Your work saves automatically every few seconds. If the window closes unexpectedly, it's there when you return.

Save & export

Download named sessions as .json to reload later. Export readable .txt files to keep a record of completed work.

Built-in guide

A full facilitation walkthrough lives inside the app — work through a session without needing anything else open.

Completely private

No account. No server. No analytics. Everything stays in your browser or on your hard drive.
