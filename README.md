## What it does

Four focuses — switch between them at the top of the app:

- **Another person** — resentment or infatuation toward someone else
- **Myself** — guilt/shame or pride/arrogance about your own conduct  
- **An event** — distress or attachment toward a situation
- **A loss or change** — grief or elation through change (Side C)
